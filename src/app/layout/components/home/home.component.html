About NationNavigator

Users can browse a comprehensive list of countries, viewing essential details like names, populations, and areas. The application presents data in an organized and easy-to-read format, allowing users to quickly find information on any country.

The sorting feature lets users arrange the list by name, population, or area. They can choose to sort in ascending or descending order, making it simple to view countries based on their preferred criteria.

The application is designed to be responsive, ensuring a smooth experience across various devices. Users can interact with dropdowns and sorting buttons seamlessly on desktops, tablets, and smartphones.

Overall, Country Finder offers a user-friendly interface for exploring global country data, with flexible sorting options and a responsive design for easy navigation.
